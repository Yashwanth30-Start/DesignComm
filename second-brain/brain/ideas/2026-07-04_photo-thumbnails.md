# Idea: Photo thumbnails from linked files

**Date:** 2026-07-04
**Status:** Deferred (Low), blocked on a decision
**Source:** YAS-15 (Phase 1 leftover, docs/ROADMAP.md)

Currently `photos` on an asset is just a **count** with placeholder icons — no real
images. To render real thumbnails, we first need to decide **where photo files/links
actually live** (SharePoint? FacilityGrid? a blob store?). Scope this only after that
source decision is made.
